#WinterIsComing


To my friends,


Not everyone deserves a seat at the table of our lives. It is rare for me to open up to certain people, so if you're reading this, I'm truly grateful for you. One year with you guys has been a roller coaster of emotions. Together we laughed at inside jokes, together we cried at our failures and from too much laughing. Together, we ate breakfast, lunch, dinner, and the in betweens. Together, we walked towards different buildings for our classes. Together, we studied in different condos and houses. Together, we attended incentives to save our grades. We all know we can do these things alone, but we chose not to; because we are a family. Now, as we are in our second year, despite having different schedules, we still find ourselves spending time with each other.

When there are times we reach rockbottom, always remember this: rockbottom is the foundation on which you can be remade. The truth is, pain can be reshaped and repurposed. With the right care, all wounds will heal back more reslient than before. When the heart is shattered, you can create something beautiful from the fragments. When there is some lesson, there is never a loss.

#WinterIsComing. Yes. We loved watching Game of Thrones together to the point wherein we watched season 7 first instead of studying immediately for our ANLYCHE finals. Thank you for not letting me drown in acads, for making me realize that there will always be life beyond it.

Love, Dani.
